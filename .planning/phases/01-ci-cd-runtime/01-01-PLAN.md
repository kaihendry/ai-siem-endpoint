## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-01 | Elevation of Privilege | OIDC role assumption | mitigate | Role ARN is hardcoded in workflow; `github.repository` and `github.ref` conditions should be added to the IAM trust policy (already assumed provisioned correctly) — no workflow-side change needed beyond using the correct role ARN |
| T-01-02 | Tampering | Makefile deploy target | accept | CI runs `make deploy` from the checked-out repo; repo is protected by GitHub's access controls |
| T-01-03 | Denial of Service | `--no-fail-on-empty-changeset` | accept | Adds resilience; does not increase attack surface |
| T-01-SC | Tampering | GitHub Actions action pins (`checkout@v4`, `setup-go@v5`, etc.) | mitigate | Use `@v4`/`@v5` major-version tags (not SHA pins); acceptable for this internal tool's threat model — supply chain risk low vs. complexity of SHA pinning |
